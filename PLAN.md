# Trend-to-Skill Academy Plan

## Vision
Build a community learning and knowledge-sharing platform where an AI agent researches trending AI topics every week, creates mini-courses, enables group learning, runs quiz battles, and rewards contributions.

## Goals
1. Keep teams updated on high-impact AI trends.
2. Convert trend knowledge into practical, role-specific learning.
3. Improve collaboration through group sessions and idea sharing.
4. Measure adoption and learning outcomes with clear analytics.

## Core Product Loop
1. Trend Scout Agent selects weekly AI topics.
2. Course Generator creates a short internal learning module.
3. Teams learn in groups with diagrams and interactive content.
4. Groups take quizzes and compete on leaderboards.
5. Insights and best ideas are saved as reusable org knowledge.

## Feature Pillars

### 1) Trend Scout Agent
- Mandatory weekly topic discovery from trusted sources.
- Topic scoring by business impact, urgency, and team relevance.
- Publish `Top 1-3 AI Topics of the Week`.

### 2) Auto Mini-Course Builder
- 15-30 minute course format.
- Includes:
  - Topic summary
  - Internal use cases
  - Risks/compliance notes
  - Implementation checklist
- Generates visual aids:
  - Workflow diagrams
  - Architecture maps
  - Before/after process visuals

### 3) Group Learning
- Learning circles by department/project.
- Shared learning room:
  - Course view
  - Discussion/Q&A
  - Facilitator notes

### 4) Group Quiz Battles
- Timed team-vs-team quizzes.
- Question bank from:
  - Weekly trend course
  - Internal knowledge base
- Adaptive difficulty and instant scoring.

### 5) Knowledge Share Feed
- Post tips, SOPs, experiments, and lessons learned.
- Upvotes/comments and peer validation.
- High-quality posts promoted to playbooks.

### 6) Leaderboards and Rewards
- Individual and team leaderboards (weekly/monthly/seasonal).
- XP, badges, streaks, and reward points.
- Contribution quality multipliers to discourage spam.

### 7) Learning Impact Dashboard
- Track:
  - Course completion
  - Quiz participation and scores
  - Team-level skill progress
  - Adoption signals from shared ideas

## MVP Scope (3 Sprints)

### Sprint 1 (Foundation)
- Trend Scout workflow (manual trigger + weekly schedule).
- Mini-course generation template.
- Group creation and membership.
- Basic learning feed and comments.

### Sprint 2 (Engagement)
- Quiz engine and group quiz mode.
- Leaderboard calculation (weekly).
- XP/badges/streaks baseline.
- Knowledge post moderation flags.

### Sprint 3 (Scale & Insights)
- Reward wallet and redemption rules.
- Auto-promotion of top posts to playbooks.
- Dashboard for admins/leads.
- Season reset + anti-spam scoring.

## Weekly Operating Rhythm
1. Monday: Publish AI topic(s) of the week.
2. Tuesday: Release mini-course and visuals.
3. Wednesday-Thursday: Group learning sessions.
4. Friday: Quiz battle + leaderboard update.
5. End of week: Save best ideas to org playbooks.

## Governance Rules
1. One mandatory weekly trend course.
2. Every course must include one org use case and one risk note.
3. Every team contributes at least one idea per month.
4. Content quality and freshness checks for promoted knowledge.

## Success Metrics
1. Weekly active learners.
2. Group quiz participation rate.
3. Knowledge contributions per team.
4. Repeat-question reduction in chat.
5. Quality score of approved shared knowledge.
6. Time-to-application for learned topics.

## Future Enhancements
1. Slack/Teams bot for learning nudges and quiz reminders.
2. Auto-ingestion from Notion/Drive/Confluence/SharePoint.
3. Skill graph per team and role-based learning paths.
4. Advanced recommendation engine for personalized learning plans.
